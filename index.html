THE SAFER WAY TO TRAMP







Checkpoint! The perfect app for anyone wanting to be safe out on the track.

Checkpoint allows you to input your track information such as expected departure and arrival time, which track you are walking on, tramping experience and emergency contacts.


If you don't check in after your estimated arrival time, a customisable alert message will be sent out to your emergency contact.


Create a profile to save emergency contacts, previous tracks and more!
